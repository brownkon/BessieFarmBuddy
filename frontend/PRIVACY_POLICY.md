# Privacy Policy for Maggie

**Effective Date:** April 13, 2026

Maggie ("we," "us," or "our") is an AI-powered farm assistant designed to help farmers manage their operations through voice commands and automated note-taking. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## 1. Information Collection

### Audio Data

Maggie accesses your device's microphone to listen for the "Maggie" wake word and to process your voice commands. Audio data is streamed to our processing services to be converted into text. We do not store raw audio files on our servers longer than necessary to process your request.

### Farm and User Data

We collect and store information you provide, such as:

- Farm notes and field observations.
- Organization and account information (email, name).
- Session history to provide context for your AI interactions.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information strictly to:

- Process and execute your voice commands.
- Provide a searchable history of your farm notes.
- Improve the accuracy of the AI responses for your specific farm needs.

## 3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We rely on the following third-party services to provide our core functionality:

- **OpenAI:** Used for natural language processing and understanding your commands.
- **Supabase:** Used for secure database storage and user authentication.
- **Fly.io:** Used to host our backend processing servers.

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data both in transit and at rest.

## 5. Your Rights and Control

- **Microphone Access:** You can grant or revoke microphone permissions at any time through your device's operating system settings.
- **Data Deletion:** You may contact us to request the deletion of your account and associated farm data.
